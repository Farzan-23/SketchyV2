Sketchy – Forensic Face Search Workflow

Upload a sketch or photo of a suspect, then scan your photo database and CCTV footage for possible matches. This prototype shows the interface and flow; the AI recognition engine is integrated on the backend.

Step 1 – Upload Sketch / Photo (Query Face)

Upload a facial sketch or photo. Sketchy will treat this as the query face to search against:

  • Stored suspect / reference photos
  • CCTV or other recorded video streams
Step 2 – Search for This Face in CCTV / Video

Once you have a query face, upload CCTV or video footage. Sketchy will scan frames and flag timestamps where the same face is likely to appear.

In this preview, the system uses simulated output to show how the results will be presented.
